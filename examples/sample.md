# AI Research Trends 2024

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence continues to evolve rapidly in 2024, with several key trends shaping the industry.

## Key Trends

### 1. Local-First AI

The shift towards local-first AI solutions allows users to run powerful models on their own hardware:
- **Privacy**: Data never leaves the user's machine
- **Cost**: No API fees or subscription costs
- **Control**: Full control over the AI pipeline
- **Offline**: Works without internet connection

### 2. Agentic AI Systems

Multi-agent architectures are becoming mainstream:
- Agents with specific responsibilities
- Tool-using capabilities
- Planning and reasoning
- Self-reflection and critique

### 3. Smaller, More Efficient Models

- Qwen 2.5 (7B-32B parameters)
- Llama 3.1 and 3.2
- Mistral models
- Phi-3 from Microsoft

These models can run on consumer hardware while maintaining high quality.

## Implications for Developers

Developers now have the tools to build production-grade AI applications without relying on cloud providers. This democratizes AI development and reduces barriers to entry.

## Conclusion

The future of AI is local, agentic, and accessible. Tools like Ollama and frameworks for local deployment are making this vision a reality.
